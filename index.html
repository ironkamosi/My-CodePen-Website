    Matsu

    Matsu is more than just an ordinary winery or wine, it is a contemporary project of sustainable viticulture that brings together an exclusive collection of high expression wines.

    Matsu means 'to wait' in Japanese and provides the perfect definition of an initiative that combines the Oriental culture's concern for what is natural with the most advanced techniques in biodynamic agriculture employed in European farming. All their wines are organic; the vines are grown in a completely natural way, avoiding any chemical or synthetic herbicides, insecticides and fungicides. Matsu represnts the essence of organic wine, and a 'wait' is included during the production cycle which allows it to finally reach its ideal balance.

    The Matsu Bodega collection: El Picaro, El Recio and El Viejo

    Matsu El "El Picaro" 2018/19 Toro

    Picaro means 'rogue' or 'rascal', and this rapscallion of a wine is a youthful interpretation of the Toro region's style. The grapes come from mature vines of over 50 years of age, but the wine spends only three months in oak, preserving their natural fruit profile.

    Vivid crimson-purple in the glass. Aromas are of ripe blackberries and raspberries, with subtle hints of fresh cocoa. The palate is bold yet supple, with ripe tannins and a mineral grip. Enjoy on its own or with boldly flavoured pasta dishes or spicy meat skewers.
